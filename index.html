What's the parch Linux now?

The parch Linux is exactly Arch, but the graphic arch. I mean, he uses arch's own tanks, and we've put some tanks for the kanfigs on the gatehub and the gatelab to make us comfortable. The parch Linux has a lot of similar examples, from Arco to alg, but there are differences that are not based on arch in other Linux (of course Manjaro has completely parted ways with Arch and resembles Ubuntu's story with Debian) parch Linux is currently presented with x86_64 architecture and we have 4 desktops by default. Plasma, Avsam, Sinamon and lxqt.

Now why the parch Linux?

At first, the parch Linux was supposed to be an acronym for Persian Arch, but then we decided it was a special name! I mean, it's not an acronym for something. Or in other words, our previous logo!
